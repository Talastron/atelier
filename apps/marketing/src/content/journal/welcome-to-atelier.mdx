Living well with the clothes you have chosen requires its own kind of attention. Not the
attention of acquisition, which is to say buying more, browsing more, scrolling more, but the
quieter attention of stewardship: knowing what you own, understanding what it cost you,
arranging it so that getting dressed each morning becomes a creative act rather than a forensic
one.

That is what Atelier exists for, and it is what we mean to write about here.
