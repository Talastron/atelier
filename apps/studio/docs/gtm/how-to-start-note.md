# "How to Start" — The Activation Note to Send New Users

_Last updated: 2026-07-14_
_Companion to [outreach-kit-first-cohort.md](./outreach-kit-first-cohort.md) and [linkedin-responder-replies.md](./linkedin-responder-replies.md). Send this the moment someone has access — it walks them to the **Style DNA reveal**, which is the activation that actually matters (not sign-up)._

> **Why this note exists:** the drop-off point is between sign-in and the reveal — people don't know the reveal exists or that it needs ~15 items. This note removes that ambiguity. Send it every time.

## Version A — Tier-A / invited friends (free access)

_Send after granting access via Profile → Invited Friends._

> **How to start — takes about 10 minutes 💛**
>
> 1. Go to **edit.myatelier.style** and sign in with the Google account I set you up on — you'll go straight in, no trial, no payment.
>
> 2. Open **Wardrobe → "Add your first item"** and add around 15 of your most-worn pieces. Phone photos are perfect — don't overthink it. (You can also paste a product link if you bought it online.)
>
> 3. Now the good bit: go to the **Insights** tab and tap **"Share my Style DNA"** — that's your reveal ✨ (it needs ~15 pieces to really sing.)
>
> 4. Tomorrow morning, open the **Today** tab — it'll suggest what to wear from what you've added. And the little **✦ button in the middle** is your private stylist — ask it anything.
>
> That's it. Any wobbles, just message me — happy to hop on a quick screenshare. x

## The follow-ups (if they stall)

- **Stalled at cataloguing:** _"Want me to add a few pieces with you on a quick screenshare? 10 minutes, done."_ Unscalable, decisive.
- **Reached the reveal and loved it:** _only then_ ask for the share ([outreach-kit Step 4](./outreach-kit-first-cohort.md)) — never before delight.

> ⚠️ **Handle-with-care contacts** (your boss, senior colleagues — e.g. Catherine, Jennifer): send **Version A**, but **do not** follow up chasing feedback or a share. Set them up graciously and leave them to enjoy it. The power dynamic turns any ask into an obligation.
