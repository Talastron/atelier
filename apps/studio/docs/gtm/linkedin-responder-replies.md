# Replying to Your LinkedIn Responders — Copy-Paste Kit

_Last updated: 2026-07-12_
_Companion to [outreach-kit-first-cohort.md](./outreach-kit-first-cohort.md). Use when colleagues/contacts reply to a LinkedIn post with "I'd love to try it." These are **replies to warm inbound**, not cold outreach — so they're appreciative and low-friction, but still walk the person to activation._

---

## Read this first — three rules that make or break it

1. **Tier before you reply.** A colleague saying "looks great!" is warm but not automatically your ICP (woman ~32–55, serious about clothes, buys £150+ without flinching). Sort each responder into **A**, **B**, or **Qualify** below *before* sending. It changes the message.
2. **Get them off LinkedIn.** LinkedIn is a professional headspace, not a wardrobe-app one, and it throttles link reach. Move the real conversation to **WhatsApp / IG DM / email** and ask for the **Google email they'll sign in with** (Tier-A free access is granted against that exact address).
3. **Don't lead with price. Lead with their taste.** The hook is "you're exactly who I made this for," not "it's £79."

> **Tiering cheat:** if you'd feel *awkward charging them* → **Tier A (free)**. If they'd feel *flattered to be let in early* → **Tier B (founding key)**. If you're *not sure they're a clothes person* → **Qualify** first.

---

## Tier A — colleague you trust, want free (for feedback + their card)

_Give it free via Profile → Invited Friends. You're buying honest feedback, a Style DNA card, and a testimonial — not their £79._

> Ah, this made my day — thank you 🙏 Honestly you're exactly the kind of eye I want on it early, so let me just *give* you access rather than point you at the trial.
>
> It's called Atelier: you photograph the pieces you own, it turns them into a beautiful private archive, and then it reads your taste back to you as a "Style DNA" — people get oddly obsessed with theirs. Takes about 10 minutes to set up.
>
> What's the best Google email for you? I'll switch on your access, and all I'd ask in return is your honest reaction — including what's missing.

**Then move to WhatsApp/email for the hand-hold (below).**

---

## Tier B — keen, clearly a "clothes person," pay-to-belong

_Point them at the 14-day trial + the founding price. Real intent = real validation. The founding price IS the discount — don't go below it._

> Thank you — that means a lot! You care about clothes properly, so you're bang-on who I built this for.
>
> Atelier turns the wardrobe you already own into a private archive and writes a read of your personal style back to you (the "Style DNA" card is the bit people love). It's just gone live and I'm letting the first hundred in at the founding price — **£79/year, locked for life** — with a 14-day trial first, no commitment.
>
> Want me to send you the founding key + link? Best to do it off LinkedIn — drop me your email or WhatsApp?

---

## Qualify — warm, but you're unsure they're ICP

_A supportive colleague curious about the build ≠ a wardrobe person. One gentle question saves you from polluting your trial→paid numbers with tourists. Their answer routes them to A, B, or "thanks for the support."_

> Thanks so much! Quick honest question before I set you up so I point you at the right thing — are you someone who actually enjoys the *clothes* side of things (wardrobe, capsules, cost-per-wear, that whole world)? It's built pretty specifically for people who do.
>
> Either way I appreciate you 🙏 — just want to make sure it lands for you and isn't a chore.

- **"Yes, obsessed"** → follow with **Tier A** or **Tier B**.
- **"Not really, just love what you're building"** → *"Amazing — then the biggest help would be a repost when I launch properly. I'll tell you when 🙏"* (keep them as a supporter/distribution, not a trial you'll misread).

---

## The activation hand-hold (send once they're in — this is the actual win)

_Reaching out is half. The win is them reaching the **Style DNA reveal**. Walk them there; don't assume they'll find it._

**Message 1 — setup nudge:**
> You're all set! Start by adding ~15 of your most-worn pieces — phone photos are perfect, don't overthink it. Fifteen is the point where the Style DNA card actually sings.

**Message 2 — point at the reveal (send after they've added a few):**
> Now go to **Insights → your Colour profile → Share my Style DNA**. That's the bit. Tell me what you think 👀

**Message 3 — the habit (next morning):**
> Open it tomorrow morning — it'll tell you what to wear. That's the part that sticks.

_If they stall at cataloguing:_ *"Want me to add a few pieces with you on a quick screenshare? 10 minutes, done."* Unscalable, decisive.

---

## The share ask (only AFTER they're delighted — earned, not begged)

> Okay you clearly love it 😄 — would you post your Style DNA card on your [Instagram story / grid] and tag [@atelier handle]? Genuinely the single biggest help you could give me right now. Zero pressure if it's not your thing.

- Ask **only** once they've expressed delight — never before.
- Their post = your first social proof + first cold traffic (this is what turns Track 1 into Track 2).
- **Save every card** (with permission) → re-feature as Pinterest pins and your own content.

## Handling the usual friction

| They say | You say |
|---|---|
| "Is it free?" (Tier A) | "For you, completely — I want your honest take, not your money." |
| "What's it cost?" (Tier B) | "£79/year if you're one of the first hundred — locked for life. 14-day trial first, cancel anytime." |
| "I don't have time to add everything." | "Just your 15 most-worn — 10 minutes. You don't need the whole wardrobe to see your Style DNA." |
| "I'll do it later." | "Want me to set it up *with* you for 10 minutes this week? I'll screenshare." |
| "I'm not techy." | "It's just photos of your clothes — if you can use Instagram, you're fine. I'll help." |
| "Can you just keep me posted?" (soft no) | "Of course — I'll ping you at launch. A repost then would be the best help 🙏" |

## Log every responder (so the numbers mean something)

|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Same sheet as your first cohort. **"Activated?" is the only column that decides anything** — interest that never becomes a catalogued wardrobe taught you nothing.

---

## Do / Don't

**Do:** tier before replying · move to WhatsApp/IG/email fast · grab their sign-in Google email · lead with their taste · hand-hold to the reveal · ask for the share only after delight · log every one.

**Don't:** mass-paste the same reply · lead with price · keep it on LinkedIn · treat every supportive colleague as a real trial · ask for the share before they're delighted · chase a non-responder more than once.
