# Phase 1 — "Plug & Drive" (Weeks 1–3) — Action Checklist

_Reality: the product is LIVE (site, Studio, 14-day trial, payments, Journal). No waitlist, no landing page to build. The job is to stop the conversion leaks, then drive traffic to the trial._
_Companion to: [atelier-gtm-strategy.md](./atelier-gtm-strategy.md)_

## Week 1 — Plug the leaks (fix the live funnel)

- [ ] **Add email capture to the Journal** and to the end of every essay (lives in the `atelier-website` monorepo). One quiet, on-brand field. Wire to a list you own (ConvertKit/Substack/Beehiiv — pick one).
- [ ] **Add a soft CTA to each essay end:** "See your own Style DNA → begin your 14 days." Link to the trial, not a waitlist.
- [ ] **Surface the Founding scarcity** on /pricing and the hero: "X of 100 founding keys claimed."
- [ ] **Put a sample Style DNA card on the landing/About page** as the shareable hook (Wedge C made visible).
- [ ] **Claim handles** consistently: Pinterest (business account), Instagram, TikTok. Same name, same avatar (`AtelierMark`), same bio line linking to the trial.
- [ ] **Bio line** (all platforms): _"An AI atelier for the wardrobe you already own. See your Style DNA · 14-day trial ↓"_
- [ ] **Decide the founder content cadence** you can sustain (recommend: 1 short-form video + 3 Pinterest pins per weekday, repurpose 1 Journal essay/week).

## Week 2 — Seed the share loop + start posting daily

- [ ] **Style DNA lead magnet** decided: simplest version = "DM me your 5 most-worn pieces, I'll send back your palette read as a card." Manual is fine at this stage — it's research + content.
- [ ] **First 10 Pinterest pins** published (Style DNA card mockups, palette boards, capsule guides — repurpose the Journal essays). Every pin description links to the trial or a Journal essay.
- [ ] **First 5 short-form videos** shot (TikTok + Reels): see scripts below.
- [ ] **Build-in-public thread** started on IG/TikTok ("Day 1 of launching my styling app…").
- [ ] **Newsletter post #2:** "The Style DNA experiment" — show real palette reads from week-1 DMs.

## Week 3 — Build the creator + community list (prep for Phase 2 "Seed")

- [ ] **Creator list:** identify 30 mid-tier (10k–80k) quiet-luxury / capsule / personal-style creators. Spreadsheet: handle, platform, follower count, why-they-fit, contact.
- [ ] **Community map:** 5–10 places the beachhead gathers (subreddits, FB groups, Discords). Join, read the rules, start contributing helpfully (no pitching yet).
- [ ] **Founding Member offer finalised:** price, what's locked, how many seats, invite-pass count.
- [ ] **Funnel milestone check:** track email captures + trial starts/week and which essay/platform drove them. Aim for the first handful of trials converting to Founding Members.
- [ ] **Newsletter post #3:** "What 200 wardrobes taught me" (or a behind-the-build teardown). Keep the cadence.

## Success criteria for Phase 1

- Landing page + owned email list live and collecting.
- Posting daily without burning out (sustainable cadence found).
- First measurable inbound (track email captures + trial starts/week and where they came from).
- A real creator + community target list ready for outreach.

## Ready-to-use copy

### Landing page (hero)

> **Eyebrow:** YOUR PRIVATE ATELIER
> **Headline:** Your wardrobe, finally worthy of itself.
> **Subhead:** Atelier turns the clothes you already own into a beautiful archive — reads your taste, writes the manifesto of your personal style, and tells you what to wear each morning.
> **CTA:** Begin your 14 days — and become one of the first 100 Founding Members (£79/yr, locked for life).
> **Reassurance line:** No fast-fashion noise. Just your wardrobe, beautifully understood.

### Newsletter post #1 — "Why I'm building Atelier" (outline)

1. The moment of frustration (a closet full of clothes, nothing to wear / £X of unworn pieces).
2. The insight: the problem isn't *more* clothes — it's *seeing* what you own.
3. What Atelier does, shown not told (one screenshot of the Colour Wheel / Style DNA).
4. The promise + the ask: "It's live — begin your 14 days and see your own Style DNA. First 100 lock the founding price for life."

### Short-form video scripts (5 to shoot)

1. **The reveal hook** — "I built an app that reads your wardrobe and tells you your style DNA. Here's mine." (screen-record the Colour Wheel filling in.)
2. **The manifesto** — "An AI wrote a 3-paragraph manifesto of my personal style. I was not ready." (read it aloud over b-roll.)
3. **The guilt-flip** — "I tracked every outfit for 30 days. Here's the £-per-wear of my most expensive coat." (the CPW angle, as *smart*, not frugal.)
4. **Build-in-public** — "Day 1 of launching my wardrobe app solo. No funding, no ads. Follow to watch." 
5. **The daily brief** — "Every morning my app tells me exactly what to wear. Here's today's." (Concierge in action.)

> Pin every video's caption to the trial link (myatelier.style). Repurpose each video as a Pinterest Idea Pin.
