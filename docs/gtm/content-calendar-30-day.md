# Atelier — 30-Day Founder Content Calendar

_Companion to [atelier-gtm-strategy.md](./atelier-gtm-strategy.md) and [phase-1-tease-checklist.md](./phase-1-tease-checklist.md)._

**Cadence (sustainable, founder-led):** 1 short-form video + ~3 Pinterest pins per weekday, 1 newsletter/week. Weekends = rest / batch-shoot.

**Every post ends with the same CTA:** the email-capture landing page link. **Every video** is repurposed as a Pinterest Idea Pin.

**Voice:** editorial, first-person, quiet-luxury. Write to one affluent style enthusiast, ~32–55.

## Repurposing rules (do more with less)

- **1 video → 4 assets:** TikTok, IG Reel, Pinterest Idea Pin, newsletter embed.
- **1 newsletter → 3 assets:** a carousel (IG), a thread, 2–3 standalone pins from its best lines.
- **Reader Style DNA cards = infinite content** once the share-card ships — every user who shares one is a pin you can re-feature (with permission).
- **Batch-shoot** all 5 of a week's videos in one sitting; schedule the rest.

## What to measure weekly

- Followers gained per platform (which channel compounds fastest).
- Landing-page email signups + **source** (which post/platform drove them).
- Pin saves + outbound clicks (Pinterest is your evergreen engine — watch it).
- Best-performing format → make more of it, cut the rest.
